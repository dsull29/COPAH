The Winners: Phipps and Keith
     After months of tireless planning and re-planning and re-re-planning and so on and so forth the 2001 Invitational COPAH 45 Tournament finally took place on June 21st, 2001. We used a table format which proved to work all right. (below you can find the table The original plans said that in the event of a tie the team with the highest point differential would be the winner but Tom and Ave chose not to have it that way. While it may have been the admirable thing to do the result could not have been worse for them. Phipps and Keith squashed Tom and Ave in about 20 minutes flat.
