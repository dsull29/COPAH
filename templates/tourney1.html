     A long time ago in a cafeteria not so far away there was the first COPAH Innovational 45 Tournament. It was a well devised plan that nearly broke down to a fist fight, but there is no need to dwell on that fact.

     The format called for a two bracket first round with the top two teams in each division advancing to play one another. If memory serves me right, and it probably doesn't so somebody please clear this up for me. Dave and Moles won Group A with Adam and Moosh coming in second. They then played Dave and Moles' coming out on top to advance to the finals. The Group B bracket came down to three teams in a tie for second, who knew, so they played the dreaded 6 man game. Scrib and Tim won the division and watched to see who their opponet would be out of Tom and Becky, Morgan and Shaw, and Seth and Guay - who during this game got set on a 20 while holding the 5, Jack and Ace of Hearts between them. Tom and Becky smashed the other two teams in 6 hands. Scrib and Tim then took out Becky and Tom. This left Scrib and Tim to face Dave and Moles in the finals. Scrib and Tim won, barely, in a griping 3 game set to be claimed the champions and walk away with the winner's share of the money.
