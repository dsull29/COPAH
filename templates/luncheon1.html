Luncheon 2000
Luncheon 2000 crowned two of COPAH's first and finest graduates, Denio and Ave, and cast them to the harsh outside world in search of gainful employment. The first COPAH luncheon was held at TK O'Malley's located on the New Bedford/Dartmouth line on ... I can't remember the date, little help? The event included lunch for 12, enough beer for 24, cheesy decorations, and best of all free pool, or as you may prefer "the ingredients for mayhem".
