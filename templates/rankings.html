Power Rankings
     In an effort to keep things fresh around here we have created the 45 Power Rankings. Now this is not a straight forward point system where you get a point for a win and lose points for a loss there is also a degree of difficulty involved. At the start of every tournament the partners have their current ranking averaged together, in the event that somebody does not yet have a ranking due to never playing before they are started at 1600. To then determine who the favorite is in a particular match up or, the rank factor, the difference between the two averages is divided by 400. In the event of win 1 is added that factor and multiplied by 16 this gives the total number of points gained by a team for a certain game. In the event of a loss the same thing is done only one is subtracted from the factor not added. Thoroughly confused? Anyway here are the rankings, the rank that comes up is the rank after that tournament, so the last would be your current rank.
