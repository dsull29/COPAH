Pool play went quick after Denio and Sandy showed. Six tables were in use pretty much at all times. High drama game after the Tyson sham, I mean fight. Moosh and Kim were playing Moles and Jesse in the living room when the fight came on. They suspended play during the fight and when the fight was over all eyes were on them as they would decide whether or not a 6 man game would be needed in Group B. It was something like 28/27 with Moosh and Kim needing to make a 20 to win on Moosh's deal. Jesse bids 15 and Kim passed which caused all behind her to gasp as they could all see she had a 5 and King, a solid 20 given the situtation. Moosh took the bid and managed to make the 20 for the win and Kim's 20 wouldn't have made it so we all looked dumb. This was easily the most watched game in tournament history with all teams standing around.

Jeff and Mikey swept and word was starting to spread that they were unbeatable. Becky and Kendra outlasted Denio and Dave. After dropping the first Denio and Dave made an amazing come back in the second. Kendra took a 20 to win which Denio let in but they were able to set them to take control and win game 2. Game 3 went 3-3,4-2,2-4,3-3,9-0,0-9 (you get the idea) no team ever had control in this one and Becky and Kendra were able to outlast. From what I was told the championship game went pretty much the same way with Jeff and Mikey having the upper hand.

The attention then shifted upstairs where oddly enough politics was the topic of choice. A far cry from the flower eating and porno days of the past. Here's what we learned (if anything):

  • Iraq produces 3% of OPEC's oil
  • Tom gets crazy when he discusses politics
  • Phipps wants to blow everything up
  • Bobby is French
  • Phipps and Dave will go to Iraq on the front line and Dave's driving
  • Tom and Dave are somehow friends despite no being able to agree on anyting
  • Bush wants oil for Enron because his dad couldn't kill Saddam
