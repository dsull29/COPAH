The Winners: Phipps and Keith
     Well it's over and the worse possible scenario came true Phipps and Keith won...again. With 9 teams gunning for them and them only they managed to pull it out, congratulations. The game managed to take a staggering 6 hours; due in part to a 6 man game being needed to decide a log jam a top the Division A bracket. Major changes will be needed for the next tournament to keep the overall length of time reasonable. Below you'll find a break down of the match-ups as well as all the pictures from the evening. Enjoy.

     Things looked they would wrap up very early in the first round of pool play but Keith and Phipps managed to drudge out a game that would have ended their night and forced a 6 man game to decide the top of that bracket. The ensuing 6 man game took about an hour and a half to play and took about 3 years off of my life. Phipps and Keith went out first leaving the final two teams to play for second with score 27-28 Adam and Moles. It came down to the last care where Adam and Moles were able to beat a kicker by one to advance to the next round.

     Keith and Phipps walked over Tom and Denio while Seth and Billy took Adam and Moles to 3 games for the win. All night Billy had been battling I think the plague and put on a truly gutty performance. Then things get blurry, 1) I had a lot to drink; 2) It was about 2/3 in the morning; 3) I immeadiately tried to erase the event from my memory. It came down to a third game both teams could have won and Phipps had the 5, I don't know if he was making a bid, setting a bid, or making a 30 no but it happened. Moments later Keith jumped into Phipps' arms and nearly drove the two of the thru a coffee table.
