New Year's @ The COPAH Beach House

     Screw Boston, Providence, and New York. The real happening First Night celebration was at the COPAH Beach House (Tom's Aunt's beach house - much easier to just refer to it as the COPAH Beach House). Let's take a second to run down the menu; 14 pound fried turkey, 10 pounds of homemade french fries, popcorn chicken, shrimp cocktail, the world's biggest salad, apple cake with ice cream, and Jaime's "Chocolate Balls". We had no CD player which kind of sucked it did however enable us to hear Sum 41 cover Nickelback's "How You Remind Me". Not that that has any significance, I thought it was really odd to hear a band cover a song that right now is the only single being played more than their own. It's like if Motley Crue covered "Every Rose has it Thorn" in 1988. I'll have some pictures up later in the week.
