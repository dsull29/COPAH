Team in Training
This group email was received by Bobby Guay on January 31st:

Hey everyone,

For the most of you who do not know, over this past Christmas my girlfriend's, Kelly, mother passed away after a three month battle with leukemia. For those of you who can relate to losing a parent you understand what she is going through. As hard as it is losing one family member in a short time frame it has recently been discovered that her older brother, Mike, has recently been diagnosed with a pre-leukemia condition and will undergo a very dangerous bone marrow transplant within the next several weeks. As most of you can imagine this type of news causes immediate stress to one's life and leaves a helpless feeling that is hard to overcome.

To overcome this feeling Kelly and I have signed up with the leukemia and lymphoma society and are participating in Team in Training (TNT). TNT is basically like a walk for hunger where as we find sponsors and carryout whatever event the society calls for. However, the events for TNT are highly athletic events such as triathlons and marathons. TNT donates over 75% of money raised to research with the remainder of the money going towards families affected by leukemia (such as if a person needs to go to a different area to undergo treatment then TNT will pay for the plane/bus trip as well as all accommodations). I encourage you to search the web and look up more information if interested.

We are signed up to participate in the Vermont City Marathon in Burlington, VT on May 26 of this year. We have been training for several months and have been assigned a professional coach to train us to finish the marathon (26.2 miles). We must raise $2,050.00 apiece to participate in the event. When signing up for TNT you must make an initial donation of $50 dollars per person to participate. I have already sponsored myself $100 and would appreciate if you would be able to spare anything to help this cause.

We are also in the process of planning a fundraiser in Taunton either at the VFW or the PACC. At this fundraiser we will be serving a sit down dinner (with Bar!), one or two live bands (Brick Park or Freezepop both have offered to play for free) as well as having several raffles throughout the evening. Exact date and time will be given in a later e-mail.

We would appreciate anything you could do to help us such as donate your time, donated items to be raffled (tickets, pictures, etc.), or a monetary value. If you are unable to donate any of the previous items we would really appreciate feedback such as ideas on fundraising or getting things donated. Any thing you give will be more than enough and will greatly be appreciated. If you are interested please get back to me as soon as you can and I will be able to give you the necessary information.

Thank You Very Much.
Bobby

P.S. For those of you who may be interested in running with us feel free to contact me or my girlfriend and we will be able to direct you to the necessary steps.

Thanks again.
