Karaoke
     A night at Karaoke typically includes many scorpion bowls, lesbians (yeah I said lesbians), dancing, a whole lot of singing, Ric Flair, mullets-a-plenty, and some interesting drunk talk, so who knows what the next time will bring? Even if you don't sing, you'll still be able to drink and laugh at those of us who do show off our vocal skills (or lack there of). More details and pictures from past nights out can be found below.
