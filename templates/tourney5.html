Purse: $500

Runners Up: Nick and Billy
Pictures: 45 Tourney V
Player Rankings: Post Tournament V

The Winners: Mikey and Tom
     Tournament V is in the books and it's safe to say that a good time was had by all that wished to have one. For those that didn't have a good time, there are no apologies what so ever.

     The evening began with the typical round table discussion of who would show up last; odds on favorite "Fall River Crew" was upset by Rob Scribner who arrived 17 minutes late. In the annals of COPAH lateness that is not even that bad. From there it was the most tense portion of the evening, the drawing of teams. Since the defending team was missing a player Oakley was forced to also enter his name into the hat, much to his chagrin. Hope there is no hard feelings but rules is rules.

     For the first time in tournament history a 6 man game was not required to break a tie, which made everybody happy and kept the total time of the tourney way down. Keith and Morgan swept threw group A in convincing fashion going 4-0. Tom and Mikey advanced from group A by having the head to head advantage over Becky and Chief. A game was played to decide who would go through to the second round for some reason but luckily the right team prevailed. Group B appeared to be the strong side of the bracket with a representive of all four championship teams, however the teams that advanced did not contain any of those players. AJ and Adam drew the first seed from group B by beating Nick and Billy head to head.

     As is custom in tournaments nobody paid any attention once they were eliminated. Choosing insted to play some games of 6 man, fusball, "survivor" high card, or who can catch the moth while remaining seated. But when teams emerged from the basement it was Tom and Mikey grinning wide as they captured the title and the winner's share of the money.
